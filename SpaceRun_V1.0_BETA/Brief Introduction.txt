In games:
[ESC]pause in game

[W]jump , you can jump twice(even in sky)after landing once.

[A]move left

[D]move right

[S]falling down

[LeftCtrl]use the tools

[Space]activate Ejection "System"(just a big jump)

[Enter]activate "auto money save" in one game

[PageDown]change BGM.

[PrintScreen]screenshot
